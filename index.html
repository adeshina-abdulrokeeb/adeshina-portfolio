About Me

I’m Adeshina Abdulrokeeb Eniola — a Frontend Developer and Prompt Engineer passionate about crafting digital experiences that balance clarity, performance, and human-centered design.

My journey into tech began out of curiosity and a desire to make things work beautifully on the web. I enjoy writing clean, maintainable code and creating interfaces that feel fast, intuitive, and accessible to everyone. I believe great design isn’t just about aesthetics — it’s about how seamlessly it works for real people.

Beyond frontend work, I explore the intersection of AI and product usability through prompt engineering — designing intelligent systems that automate content, enhance user workflows, and help teams make smarter decisions.

Being a Medicine & Surgery student at University of Lagos has taught me discipline, empathy, and systems thinking — qualities that now influence how I build and solve problems in code.

I’ve also trained at Grazac Academy (Frontend Development) and Data Science Nigeria’s 3MTT Program (Generative AI). These experiences shaped my approach to combining structure with creativity — whether I’m writing CSS or designing prompt flows.

I build with intent: pragmatic, testable solutions. Clean markup, small bundles, and prompt chains that include validation steps. The goal? Reliable UIs and predictable AI outputs — tools that teams can trust in production.
